Pasta With Meat Sauce

Aldente pasta noodles topped with a tomato based meat sauce.

I find the versitility of pasta to be one of its best attributes. It can be prepared in such a variety of ways. It is similar to rice in that respect. Both are starches as well. This specific pasta dish will consist of spaghetti noodles with a tomato based meat sauce. It'sa spicy meatbol ;o

This dish is heavy and will put some meat on your bones so be careful if you're watching your weight. Great recipe for getting swoled up though.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles(any dry pasta will work)
  • 1lb of Meat
  • Classico Pasta Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Add six cups of water to a large pot or saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Once a rolling boil is achieved add dry pasta. Ammount will vary based on portion size
  3. Allow pasta to boil for 11 minutes or until it becomes aldente texture, then drain.
  4. While pasta is boiling begin cooking your meat. If meat is pre-cooked skip this step.
  5. Now it is time to combine your cooked meat and pasta with the tomato sauce. Combine in large pot and stir.
  6. It is now ready to serve.
